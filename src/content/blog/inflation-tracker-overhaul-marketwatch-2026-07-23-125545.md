## Key concepts explained

### Inflation tracker

A tool or index used to monitor the rate at which prices rise across the economy. It helps observers gauge inflation trends and informs expectations about policy actions.

### Monetary policy expectations

The market’s view on what central banks will do with interest rates based on current and expected economic data.

### Methodology overhaul

A change in how an indicator is calculated or presented, which can shift readings even if underlying price movements are similar.

### Data revision

Updates to previously published data or the inclusion of new data sources that alter historical readings.
