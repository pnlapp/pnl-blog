Becoming a smarter trader who learns from mistakes and improves day by day takes more than information alone. It takes clear concepts, structured learning, and materials that help you actually apply what you study.

That is why PnL App built a set of trading education materials. On Gumroad, you can explore free previews, full guides, and practice tests designed to make learning more practical and easier to follow. Whether you are just getting started or reviewing important concepts, these resources are built to help you learn with more clarity and confidence.

The collection includes a free preview with the first two chapters from each guide, complete learning bundles for deeper study, and practice workbooks with explanations so you can test your understanding instead of only reading passively.

## What the bundle covers

- **Investing: Where to Start.** A beginner's guide to building knowledge, strategy, and confidence in the markets.
- **Fundamental Analysis.** How to read what a business, its finances, and its trends actually mean.
- **Technical Analysis.** How to read charts, identify levels, and build a repeatable process.
- **Practice Workbook.** Questions and explanations to test whether the concepts actually stuck.

The goal is simple: make trading and investing education more accessible, less overwhelming, and more useful in the real world.

You can explore the full collection on [Gumroad](https://pnlapp.gumroad.com/).
