## Key concepts explained

The EURUSD is the exchange rate between the euro and the U.S. dollar. Movements in this pair reflect relative demand for euros and dollars driven by economic data, policy expectations, and risk sentiment.

The ECB policy decision refers to the central bank's actions or guidance on monetary policy, including interest rate changes or signaling future policy shifts. The decision can influence currency markets through changes in rate expectations and outlook for inflation and growth.

### EURUSD dynamics
